Charmander

Type: Fire

Evolves into: Charmeleon (at level 16), then into Charizard (at level 36)

Description: Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon with a flame on its tail. It's a popular choice among trainers due to its eventual evolution into Charizard, a powerful Fire/Flying-type Pokémon. Charmander's signature move is "Ember."
