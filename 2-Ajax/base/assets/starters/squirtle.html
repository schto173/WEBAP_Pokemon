Squirtle

Type: Water

Evolves into: Wartortle (at level 16), then into Blastoise (at level 36)

Description: Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon recognizable by its blue shell and turtle-like appearance. It's known for its water-based attacks and its eventual evolution into Blastoise, a Water-type Pokémon with powerful water cannons on its back.
