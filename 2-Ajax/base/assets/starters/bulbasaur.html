Bulbasaur

Type: Grass/Poison

Evolves into: Ivysaur (at level 16), then into Venusaur (at level 32)

Description: Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon with a plant bulb on its back. It's known for its balanced abilities and its ability to learn a variety of moves. Bulbasaur is the first step in the evolutionary line of powerful Grass-type Pokémon.
